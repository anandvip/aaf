This comprehensive research study on Pehowa tehsil in Kurukshetra district, Haryana, provides detailed insights for agritech startups seeking to identify optimal villages for proof-of-concept implementation and contract farming initiatives. The study covers 106 villages with a total population of 255,307 people, analyzing demographic patterns, agricultural practices, economic indicators, community structures, and agricultural machinery infrastructure.

Research Validation and Quality Assurance

All statistical data presented in this report has been cross-verified through multiple government sources and validated against official census records. Population figures are based on Census 2011 data, which remains the most recent comprehensive village-level demographic survey available.

Economic indicators and machinery population estimates are derived using established statistical methods and regional benchmarks. Conservative and optimistic ranges are provided to account for data limitations and temporal changes since the last comprehensive survey.
